I DEFINITELY didn’t do it. 

Characters:

Criminal 

Hurt Person: 

Larlie 

Victor

Keeth

Criminal and Hurt Person struggle and stage fight. Criminal punches Hurt Person to the ground leaving them unconscious sprawled across the floor. Criminal suspiciously runs away. Victor and Keeth are walking by, spot Hurt Person sprawled on the floor, and rush over to help them.

Victor

To Keeth

Oh my god! Is that guy okay??

Keeth checks Hurt Guy’s pulse

Keeth

He’s still got a pulse. I think he’s just unconscious?

Victor

He looks pretty beat up. 

Keeth

Yeah, god, I can't believe people would just leave someone here like this

Larlie walks in the opposite direction Victor and Keeth did: weirdly suspicious, scratching his head with one finger

Larlie

Hmm, yeah I wonder who did that. 

Keeth and Victor look at Larlie suspiciously? 

Victor

..um yeah. Isn’t it awful?

Larlie

Yup!!! Suuuuuuuuper awful. I would definitely never do anything like that. 

Keeth

Yeah… I don’t think most people would… Sorry. Do you know anything about what happened here?

Larlie

What? Me???? Nahhh psshhhh. No. 

Victor and Keeth continue to look at each other bewilderedly

Victor

No, it really seems like you know. 

Keeth

Yeah you’re being.. Really weird about this. 

Larlie

Whaaaaaaat? I have no idea what you’re talking about

Almost distressed now

Larlie

I’m serious I definitely don’t know how that guy ended up on the floor

Victor

I think I’m gonna call the police. 

Larlie

Larlie starts to step towards Victor and KEETH.

High pitched and almost like begging but in the wrong??? Way? 

No, no, you definitely don’t have to do that

KEETH

Hey. STAY BACK.

Hurt Person

murmuring

Ufdsahlf what’s happening

Victor bends down to help hurt person get up. Simultaneously Criminal walks onto the scene acting similarly to how Victor and Keeth are: NORMAL

Criminal 

Woah, is that guy okay? Do you want me to call someone? 

Larlie

No noo… Nah,,,,,,, he’s fine!!!! Guys everything is fine!!!

Victor gets up and Hurt Person just starts to look around, regaining consciousness. He stares at the criminal for a bit. Victor Keeth and Criminal have all positioned themselves opposite of Larlie

simultaneously

Victor : Keeth : Criminal 

No, this man was just attacked : A crime has just been committed… : I can’t believe anyone would do something like this

Hurt Person

Pointing at criminal guy

HEY! You're the person that hit me!!!

Criminal Guy looks around, scratching his neck, pretending he doesn’t know what hurt person is talking about. 

Larlie

In the same confusing, guilty, tone as Larlie

Whaaa??? Nooooo, pshhhh I definitely didn’t do thaaaat.
