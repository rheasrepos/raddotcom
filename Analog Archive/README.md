# Analog Archive
Scanned / physical work — kept separate from your digital writing (which lives in "Recovered Writing").

## How to use
- Scan or photograph a physical item, then drop it into the matching folder (or **00 INBOX** if unsure — I can sort it later).
- Naming tip: `YYYY-MM-DD short description.jpg/pdf` (e.g. `2019-05 poetry-notebook-page.jpg`). A date helps it slot into your timeline.
- Multi-page items: a single PDF is ideal; loose pages are fine too.

## Folders
- **00 INBOX (unsorted scans)** — dump here when unsure; I'll file it.
- **Handwritten writing & journals** — drafts, journal pages, handwritten poems/essays.
- **Art & sketches** — drawings, paintings, design work.
- **Letters & cards** — correspondence worth keeping.
- **Printed - zines, publications, programs** — anything published/printed (show programs, zines, newspaper clippings).
- **Notebooks** — full notebook scans.
- **Photos of physical work** — photos of 3-D or large pieces.

When you've added scans, tell me and I can date/organize them, and (later) wire selected ones into the site alongside your written pieces.
